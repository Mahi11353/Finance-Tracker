Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the Service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your information from unauthorised access.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes.

We may use trusted third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to run the application. These providers process your data only on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
